Miled El Ghazel Center (MGC), is a both cultural and sporty center that offers many physical activities for both children and adults, and cultural activities from time to another such as theatre acting, summer events for kids and a cinema. This establishment was opened in 2002 in Zgharta, north lebanon. People from different regions and nearby towns comes to participate in their activities.

Here's what our Center offers:

Taekwondo
Learn Taekwondo and either choose to become a champion and partake in tournaments or use this knowledge for self defence. classes for both adults and kids.

Gym
A Gym with equipments for trainning all body parts along with two expert coaches to guide those who have never been to a gym before

Basketball
Basketball classes for both kids and adults, you may also rent the Basketball playground.

Oriental Dance
learn the traditional Middle Eatern oriental dance through this class

Fitness Class
Fitness classes for those who wants to stay in shape or sculpt their dream bodies.

Gymnastic
Gymnastic class created for kids, which allows them to learn various acrobatic skills and develope their flexibilty from early age.

Thai Boxing
Thai Boxing Classes for both adults and kids, where you can learn useful self defence techniques or become a Thai Boxing expert.
